About CreativesAtWork

Through dedicated services to a global client base,
CreativesAtWork aims to be a global job marketplace for media freelancers and project owners.
We can help you simplify all your media projects!

Our Services

Matchmaking

For project owners, CreativesAtWork provides an efficient and effective selection process to identify the most suitable freelancers for their media projects.

For media freelancers, CreativesAtWork provides an aggregating platform for their profiles and expands their job opportunities.

Project Management

To simplify the process of engaging the freelancers especially in more complex creative products, we will be happy to assist you in ensuring the smooth production of the creative process all the way to final delivery.

Leave the hassle to us, we provide high quality services to ensure your project are done by the most qualify freelancers and will be delivered on time.

What We Do?

Based in Asia, CreativesAtWork is a media agency that brings together project owners and media freelancers from all over the world. With a strong network of media freelancers, CreativesAtWork provides a complete solution to every project owner's media needs and requirements. From corporate branding and graphic design, to video production and post-production, to photography and talents, CreativesAtWork covers the entire chain of media skills.

For project owners, CreativesAtWork provides an efficient and effective selection process to identify the most suitable freelancers for their media projects. For media freelancers, CreativesAtWork provides an aggregating platform for their profiles and expands their job opportunities.
